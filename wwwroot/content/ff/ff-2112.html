When breastfeeding doesn't work out

This video is about when breastfeeding doesn't work out. It can be hard to decide to stop breastfeeding and start bottle-feeding, and it's important for you to feel supported. A counsellor and a child and family health nurse give tips for bonding with your baby while bottle-feeding and getting support; mums who have been through this talk about the support they got from mothers groups and counsellors, as well as from friends and family.

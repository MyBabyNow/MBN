Off and running

Screen time (such as the TV, tablets, phones, computers etc) is not recommended at all for children under 2 years of age because it can affect their development.

Besides, when the screens are off, your child has more opportunity to be off and running.

Even if they're not able to stand or walk just yet, by encouraging your baby to be active every day, you're boosting your baby's learning and development!

Don't forget, your baby is always watching you and trying to copy what you do, so by reducing your own screen time and being active yourself, you're also demonstrating to your baby that this is normal behaviour!

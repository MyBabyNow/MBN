Eat together, play together

Because your baby is always watching you and trying to copy what you do, you are your baby's most important role model! Remember, baby see, baby do!

So, rather than telling your baby one thing but doing something different yourself, the best way to raise your child with good habits is to make sure you also practice good habits.

A simple way to practice healthy habits is by enjoying meal times together as often as you can and making time every day for some active play with your baby.

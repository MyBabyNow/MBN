Snack on fruit and vegetables

Eating a wide range of fruits and vegetables is one of the most important things we can all do for our health!

But rather than trying to squeeze our recommended daily serves of fruit and vegetables just into meals, make sure you and your baby take advantages of snacks.

It's a good idea to think of snacks as mini-meals which give us another opportunity to eat a variety of foods from all the food groups!

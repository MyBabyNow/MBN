Breastfeeding is a learning curve - we're in it together!

There are many benefits to breastfeeding, and not just for you and your baby! Although breastfeeding is natural, it is still a skill that you and your baby need to learn, in order to do it successfully. And just like any new skill, this is something that takes time, patience and persistence. In fact, it can take a mum and her baby 6-8 weeks to figure out what works best for them!

It's quite common to find breastfeeding hard, which is why there's plenty of help available - much of which is free! So instead of feeling alone when it comes to breastfeeding challenges - make sure you reach out!

Our breastfeeding section is designed to help you every step of the way, but if you need to talk to someone right now, don't forget there are always breastfeeding counsellors - who are also mums that have been through it all before - waiting to take your call on the Australian Breastfeeding Association helpline 1800 686 268 - 24 hours a day, 7 days a week!

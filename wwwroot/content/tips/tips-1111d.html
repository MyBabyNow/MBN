Start a sippy cup from 6 months, plan to stop using bottles by 12 months

Once your baby reaches 6 months of age, it's a good idea to start offering your baby's drinks in a 'sippy' cup - a training cup that helps your baby learn how to sip (which will eventually help them learn to talk!).

The aim is to introduce a cup at 6 months and if you're bottle feeding to stop using bottles altogether by 12 months. Keep in mind that cooled boiled water or breastmilk / formula are the only suitable drinks to put in the cup.

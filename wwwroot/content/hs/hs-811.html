Emergency contacts

You will hopefully never need to use any of these services but it is always a good idea to know what's available and how to use it, just in case.

  • Ambulance, fire or police
  • Child protection
  • Family violence
  • Poisons
  • Sexual assault
  • Suicide risk

Ambulance, fire or police

To contact Police, Fire or Ambulance in life threatening or emergency situations.

Triple Zero

  • Is someone seriously injured or in need of urgent medical help?
  • Is your life or property being threatened?
  • Have you just witnessed a serious accident or crime?

If you answered YES call Triple Zero (000).

Help and support - an overview

This section is all about helping you find the contacts and support you need, when you need it. Life as a soon-to-be or new parent is exciting but can sometimes feel overwhelming, tiring or even scary! But it doesn't have to be - there is plenty of support available to you, some is even available 24 hours a day... Look for this symbol: .

Flick through this section to familiarise yourself with some of the services available. Unless otherwise stated, everything is available Australia-wide. Don't forget to connect with other expecting and new parents through the online forums and keep coming back as your parenting journey unfolds.

In this section you will find help and support grouped by:

  • Emergency contacts
  • Pregnancy
  • Parenting
  • General health
  • Feeding
  • Sleep advice and services
  • Play and development
  • Help for you
  • Online forums
  • Other contacts

Just keep in mind that many services provide support in more than one area!

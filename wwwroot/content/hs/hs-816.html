Sleep advice and services

In addition to the services listed here, you can also refer to the parenting section for plenty of other services (including state-specific services) which can also provide advice during pregnancy.

Red Nose
Service dedicated to preventing the sudden and unexpected deaths of babies and children during pregnancy, infancy and their pre-school years. Resources include interactive website with information categorised by stage of pregnancy/baby age - e.g. standards for cots/prams, safe sleeping and tummy time.

Website

New South Wales

Karitane ®
A charity service for children and their parents from birth to 5 years that provides parenting support and advice, antenatal support and education, services to help with parental depression and anxiety, managing toddler behaviour and other related issues. Resources include a website with information on parenting and settling techniques and sleep promotion, helpline and residential, day and outreach services.

1300 227 464 (12:30pm-9pm; 11pm-6:30am Mon-Thu; Fri-Sat 9am-3:30pm)
Website

Tresillian Family Care Centres
An early parenting service offering families guidance in the early years of their child's life. Resources include information on parenting and settling techniques as well as sleep promotion, telephone and online helpline, community outreach and face-to-face advice.

1300 272 736) (7am-11pm/7 days)
Live Advice Website

Victoria

Mercy Health O'Connell Family Centre
Services directed towards families experiencing complex issues and who require increased support in addition to the support provided by the professionals in the local community. Resources include a website with details of parenting sessions including sleeping and settling.

03 8416 7600 (24 hours/7 days)
Website

Tweddle Child and Family Health Service
An early intervention and prevention health service which provides assistance to families in north and west Victoria during pregnancy and with children up to school age that are facing multiple challenges. Note - referral from a health care professional required. Resources include a website with 'Tweddle Tip Sheets' (parenting information including settling techniques and sleep promotion), residential parenting courses and psychology services.

03 9689 1577
Website

Playgroup

Playgroup is an informal session where mums, dads, grandparents, caregivers, children and babies meet up in a relaxed and friendly environment. It is an opportunity for your baby to play with other babies and for you to meet other parents in your locality (annual fee for membership). Resources include a website detailing where to find a playgroup in your area as well as how to conduct a playgroup, online shop selling books about parenting/play etc and online membership sign up.
